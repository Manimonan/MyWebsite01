Digital Literacy

Understanding Digital Literacy

Digital literacy refers to the skills and knowledge required to use digital technologies effectively and safely. It includes understanding how to operate devices, navigate the internet, evaluate online information, communicate digitally, and protect personal data. In today's world, digital literacy is essential for education, employment, and everyday tasks.

Why Digital Literacy Matters

  • Helps individuals use smartphones, computers, and digital tools confidently.
  • Enables safe browsing and awareness of cyber threats.
  • Improves communication through email and online platforms.
  • Boosts job opportunities and digital workplace skills.
  • Allows access to online learning, banking, and government services.
Tip: Always verify information before sharing it online. Digital responsibility is part of digital literacy.
