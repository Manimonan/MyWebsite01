Overview & Purpose of Examination

The SSC GD Constable Examination is one of the most popular competitive exams conducted by the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) for recruitment in Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs). Every year, lakhs of candidates apply for this exam to serve the nation through forces like BSF, CISF, CRPF, ITBP, SSB, NIA, SSF, and Assam Rifles.

This article provides a complete and updated guide on SSC GD Constable 2025 including eligibility criteria, syllabus, exam pattern, latest vacancy details, and the best books for preparation.

The primary purpose of the SSC GD exam is to select physically fit, mentally strong, and disciplined candidates for Indiaâ€™s security forces. It ensures recruitment of capable youth to strengthen national security.

Eligibility Criteria

Nationality:

  • Candidate must be a citizen of India.

Educational Qualification:

  • Matriculation (Class 10) pass from a recognized board

Age Limit:

  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Maximum Age: 23 years
  • Age relaxation applicable for SC/ST/OBC/Ex-servicemen as per government rules.

Physical Standards:

  • Height, chest, and endurance requirements vary for male and female candidates.
  • Physical Efficiency Test (PET) and Physical Standard Test (PST) are mandatory.

Syllabus

  • General Intelligence & Reasoning: Analogies, Similarities & Differences, Spatial Visualization, Coding-Decoding, Logical Reasoning
  • General Knowledge & Awareness: Current Affairs, History, Geography, Indian Constitution, Science & Technology
  • Elementary Mathematics: Number System, Percentages, Ratio & Proportion, Averages, Profit & Loss, Mensuration
  • English / Hindi: Comprehension, Grammar, Vocabulary, Sentence Structure
